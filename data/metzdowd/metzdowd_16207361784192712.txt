4) Long-duration (11 ms) shock capability of 150 Gs

1) Operating temperatures of 5&#176; to 55&#176;C (41&#176; to 131&#176;F)
2) Operating altitudes from &#8211;1,000 ft to 10,000 ft (&#8211;300 m to 3,000 m)
3) Operating vibration up to 0.5 Gs
4) shock capability of 100 Gs
